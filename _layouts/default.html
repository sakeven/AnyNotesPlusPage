What The App Produces

Results that are easier to use than raw notes.

Inbox result types

Structured outputs for common note shapes.

  • Idea: clarify a thought, pull out key points, and suggest what to explore next.
  • Meeting: summarize the discussion, decisions, action items, and unresolved questions.
  • Reminder: turn a quick note into a task-ready draft instead of leaving it as text.
  • Summary: compress web pages, chats, and reference material into the main takeaways.
  • Knowledge: turn highlights and research notes into something easier to reuse later.

What you can do next

Move from understanding to execution without leaving the flow.

  • Continue the note in AI Chat with note context already attached.
  • Draft reminders, Things items, and follow-up emails from the AI result.
  • Generate a shareable image card instead of sending a raw screenshot.
  • Search saved notes, batch summarize multiple notes, or archive and pin for later review.
  • Use custom prompts and the sandbox workspace when the task needs more than a single reply.

Private by default

Your notes stay tied to your own account.

Notes live in your personal iCloud-backed storage instead of a shared service database, so capture can stay lightweight without adding a new account system.

Built for ongoing work

Not just capture, but progression.

The app keeps the quick-entry speed of a note inbox while adding search, archive, topics, AI chat, and shareable outputs for the notes that need to turn into something real.
